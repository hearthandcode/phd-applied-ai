*Annotated reading list generated in Workflow Phase B/C/D.*

- **docs/open-library/pedagogy/how-people-learn-brain-mind-experience-and-school-expanded-edition-the-national.md** — How People Learn: Brain, Mind, Experience, and School (National Academies): The most comprehensive synthesis of cognitive science and learning theory for educational application.
- **docs/open-library/cognitive-science-learning/situated-learning-legitimate-peripheral-participation-jean-lave-and-etienne-weng.md** — Situated Learning: Legitimate Peripheral Participation (Lave and Wenger): Foundational text on situated cognition and community-of-practice learning theory.
- **docs/open-library/cognitive-science-learning/cognitive-processes-brain-and-cognitive-sciences-mit-opencourseware.md** — Cognitive Processes (MIT Brain and Cognitive Sciences, OCW): MIT course covering memory, attention, and reasoning as core learning-relevant cognitive processes.

- **docs/open-library/cognitive-science-learning/the-nature-of-constructionist-learning-media-arts-and-sciences-mit-opencoursewar.md** — The Nature of Constructionist Learning (MIT Media Arts and Sciences, OCW): Papert's constructionist learning theory, a cornerstone of cognitive science applied to education.
