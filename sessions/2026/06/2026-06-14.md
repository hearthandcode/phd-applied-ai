### [~11:28 EDT] AI as re-engagement catalyst — motivational identity reflection

**Type:** motivational
**RQ:** RQ5
**Tags:** meta-cognition · hyperfocus · low-energy-productive

Subject described a six-year period of intellectual disengagement (passive consumption, goalless activity) followed by a recent resurgence of intellectual energy explicitly correlated with AI tool access. Identified mechanism: AI closes the conception-to-execution gap — the conceptual layer (ideas, systems thinking) functions well; the implementation layer (where to start, what the steps are) was where signal was lost.
