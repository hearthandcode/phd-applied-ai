## How posts connect to modules

Each post in `modules/M##-*/blog-post-seed.md` has:
- The seed scaffold (generated by workflow)
- `published_date` and `substack_url` fields (filled when published)

When you publish a post, update:
1. The module's `blog-post-seed.md` front matter (`status: published`, urls)
2. This file (`POSTS.md`) with the new row
